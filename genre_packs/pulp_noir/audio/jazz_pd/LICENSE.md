# Public Domain Jazz Collection

Source: Internet Archive — "A CD's Worth of 1920s, '30s Jazz & Dance 4"
URL: https://archive.org/details/1920sJazz_201606
License: Public Domain Mark 1.0

These recordings are 78 RPM transfers from the 1920s-1930s. Under the
Music Modernization Act (2018), sound recordings published before 1923
entered the US public domain on January 1, 2022. Recordings from
1923-1928 entered on January 1, 2024.

These are free to use, copy, modify, distribute, and perform for any
purpose, including commercial use, without permission.

Attribution: Original artists credited in filenames and track metadata.
